About the Programme

Let your imagination and technical skills soar with our B.Tech in Computer Science & Engineering (Specialisation in Animation & VFX). This program for dreamers, innovators, and problem-solvers combines the strengths of computer science and engineering with the infinite possibilities of animation and visual effects.

This program seeks to equip students with a strong background in computer science supplemented by specialist knowledge in Animation and Visual Effects (VFX) to keep up with the changing needs of the media and entertainment industry. It prepares students with the technical and creative expertise required for animation production, VFX compositing, 3D asset creation, and post-production. By mixing computer programming, AI-based animation technology, and professional software training, the program readies graduates for employment in film, television, gaming, digital media, and beyond.

Equipped with cutting-edge studios, industry-standard software, and hands-on tutorials, you'll produce cinematic masterworks, interactive games, and hyper-realistic virtual worlds. Whether you aspire to code clever animation systems, create breathtaking effects, or revolutionise digital experiences, the program unlocks unlimited possibilities.

Come and turn your passion into a career—where technology and imagination meet, and the possibilities are endless!
